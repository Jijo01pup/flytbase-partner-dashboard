Partner Intelligence Feed Logic

The feed is Shruti's daily action list. Every item answers: what do I do, for whom, and why right now. It reads from the partner database and is refreshed every weekday at 8AM IST from FlytForce call notes.

Can offer
Market-entry materials — vertical guides, proof packs, case studies, competitive positioning decks, one-pagers, localised collateral

Does NOT do

LinkedIn targeting or paid media
Demand-gen campaigns on behalf of partners
